About Me

Learn more about my journey, skills, and passions!

Early Life

Born and raised around Colombo, I spent my early years schooling at one of the leading Catholic schools in the area. My education was balanced out by extra curriculars as well; participating in sports such as Wushu and Basketball helped improve my team spirit. Besides that, being Vice Captain of Debating Club at school gave me that straightforward edge to my personality.

After passing Ordinary Levels with flying colours, I chose to pursue the Mathematics Stream for Advanced Levels. After passing Advanced Levels, I turned down the offer to attend the University of Peradeniya, I decided to accept a place in IIT's Infoschol programme due to the opportunity to get real industry exposure.

Technical Skills

Hobbies and Interests

Because I believe that having a healthy life outside of work is essential for productivity and overall wellbeing, I enjoy taking part in a variety of diverse hobbies.

After discovering my passion for public speaking, I became active within my university's Toastmasters Club, even serving for their secretary as a year. I cherish the relationships and networking opportunities this has brought me. Outside of that, I enjoy music as well as engaging in other artistic endeavours such as writing, reading and drawing. But I also believe that it's important to keep yourself active, especially in this sedentary field that we have chosen, which is why I try to visit the gym at least three times a week and I love playing badminton with my friends.
